Remarks

  • Headings: outline of the page, H1 only once
  • Neutral background: low saturation
  • Font combination: serif and sans-serif fonts
  • No more than 3 fonts
  • Important and short information: display / handwriting fonts
  • Catching attention: increased font weight, accent color
  • Different states of buttons
